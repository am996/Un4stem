UN4STEM is a nonprofit organization connecting high school mentors with students to expand access to STEM education through peer-led learning and structured programs.

Our Mission

We aim to close educational gaps in STEM by creating accessible, student-led learning environments where mentorship drives understanding.

Mentorship

High school students teach STEM topics in a relatable way.

Structured Learning

Organized courses across math, science, and computing.

Accessibility

Programs designed for both online and in-person learning.

Student Perspective

Built by students who understand learning challenges firsthand.
