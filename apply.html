Volunteer for our Student-Led Non-profit

Join our student-led initiative and help expand access to STEM education through peer mentorship.

We are actively seeking motivated high school leaders who are passionate about science and education. As a mentor, you will have the opportunity to shape the academic journey of younger students while developing your own leadership and communication skills within a professional nonprofit framework.


After applying, our team reviews each submission and matches students with appropriate courses or teaching opportunities based on interest and experience.
